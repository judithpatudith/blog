Tech 4 N00bs

Tech is confusing for beginners like me

Bulit by humans, still confusing.

My training is in evolutionary developmental biology, a field where randomness rules. When I got a job in tech, I was excited to work with logical systems built by humans.

But I’m slowly discovering that the logic I had hoped for isn’t always obvious, especially to beginners like me. So here I set out to explain things as I figure them out.
